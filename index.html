Latest News

After the Dreamworks closure, I completed a 3 month immersive full stack web development "boot camp" class at General Assembly. While there I created an individual project called FitBike. Within three weeks of being deployed, the app proved to be popular with performance minded cyclists with over 200 users and 400 bikes in the database, and it's growing. Since adding an option for kilometers, I now have users from all over the globe! The app leverages the Strava API to allow cyclists to know when service is needed on their bikes. My GA training led me to a position at Pandora. Since December of 2015, I've been working as an Application Engineer supporting large software teams.You can see much of my recent code on GitHub.
